MANALI

Location

Manali, Himachal Pradesh

Activities

Trekking
Rock Climbing
Paragliding

Best Time to Visit

Embark on your mountain odyssey between May and October, when Manali unveils its hidden treasures amidst clear skies and blooming landscapes.

Time Required

Prepare for a heart-pounding 3-5 days of conquering the untamed heights and soaring to new horizons.

Entry Fee

Embrace the challenge with trekking permits and paragliding experiences starting at INR 500, where the thrills are worth every penny.

Accomodation

Retreat to the cozy haven of Solang Valley Resort, where you'll be embraced by the majestic mountains.

Restaurants

Johnson's Café - Let your taste buds dance to the melody of delectable cuisines in a serene ambiance.
Chopsticks - Savor the spices of Asia while feasting your eyes on panoramic mountain vistas.

How to Reach

Embark on a journey of wonder by flying into Bhuntar Airport or embark on a scenic road trip from Delhi to Manali. Choose the path that calls to your adventurous spirit.

About

Unveil the adventurer within you as Manali opens its arms to you. Trek through lush forests, conquer rocky cliffs, and let the wind carry you high with paragliding. At Solang Valley Resort, bask in the grandeur of nature, while Johnson's Café and Chopsticks tantalize your palate with flavors as diverse as the mountains themselves.





DARJEELING

Location

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Activities

Mountaineering
Rappelling
Cable Car Rides

Best Time to Visit

Answer the call of Darjeeling's mountains between March and May or October and December, when the valleys come alive with adventure.

Time Required

Prepare for a mountain escapade of 4-6 days, where each moment leaves you breathless with wonder.

Entry Fee

Conquer the peaks with permits for mountaineering and cable car rides, paying homage to the mountains starting at INR 1000.

Accomodation

Surrender to the charm of Mayfair Darjeeling, where the mountains embrace you with open arms.

Restaurants

Glenary's - Taste history in every bite as you feast on the delights of this iconic bakery and café.
Keventers - Sip on the essence of Darjeeling amidst breathtaking mountain views from the terrace.

How to Reach

Let your journey ascend to new heights by flying into Bagdogra Airport and journeying to Darjeeling amidst the picturesque vistas.

About

Dare to dream and reach for the stars as Darjeeling welcomes you to its mountains' embrace. Conquer the heights, descend sheer cliffs, and soar high with cable car rides. Mayfair Darjeeling offers you a cozy mountain retreat, while Glenary's and Keventers indulge your senses with flavors that mirror the region's enchanting beauty.





LADAKH

Location

Ladakh, Jammu, and Kashmir

Activities

Hiking
Ice Climbing
Camel Safaris

Best Time to Visit

Unravel the magic of Ladakh between June and September, when the mountain realms unveil their captivating beauty.

Time Required

Embark on a mountain odyssey of 5-7 days, where every step reveals a new chapter of awe-inspiring vistas.

Entry Fee

Unlock the mysteries with hiking permits and camel safari experiences, paying homage to the mountains starting at INR 500.

Accomodation

Be pampered by The Grand Dragon Ladakh, where modern comforts blend harmoniously with ancient landscapes.

Restaurants

Bon Appetit - Indulge in a symphony of flavors, celebrating Ladakh's essence in every bite.
Tibetan Kitchen - Delight in culinary tales of Tibet while dining in an enchanting atmosphere.

How to Reach

Embark on a journey of a lifetime by flying into Leh Airport and venturing through Ladakh's rugged terrains, or immerse yourself in the awe-inspiring beauty of the region via the enchanting Manali-Leh Highway.

About

Prepare to be mesmerized by Ladakh, where adventure knows no bounds. Hike through landscapes that seem straight out of dreams, conquer icy cliffs, and ride camels across vast deserts. The Grand Dragon Ladakh offers a warm abode amid the mountain grandeur, while Bon Appetit and Tibetan Kitchen take you on a culinary expedition that captures Ladakh's heart and soul. Surrender to the embrace of the mountains and unlock the spirit of adventure that lies within you!
