GOA

Location

Goa

Activities

Jet Skiing
Parasailing
Scuba Diving

Best Time to Visit

From October-April, Goa beckons with sunny skies and calm waters, creating the perfect playground for aquatic enthusiasts.

Time Required

Get ready for a whirlwind half-day adventure, jam-packed with adrenaline-pumping watersports.

Entry Fee

Embrace the thrill with prices ranging from INR 500 to INR 2500 for each activity, depending on your chosen package.

Accomodation

Retreat to the lavish embrace of Radisson Blu Resort Goa Cavelossim Beach, where luxury and beachside charm unite.

Restaurants

Fisherman's Wharf - A culinary treasure trove offering lip-smacking Goan delicacies amidst a delightful ambiance.
Martin's Corner - Savor the blend of international flavors and local spices at this hotspot loved by travelers and locals alike.

How to Reach

Arrive in style via Dabolim Airport or hop on a train to Madgaon Railway Station. From there, taxis and rental cars will whisk you away to the sun-kissed shores.

About

Goa isn't just about its vibrant nightlife; it's a paradise for water lovers too! Jet ski through the waves, feel the rush of soaring high during parasailing, and unlock the secrets of the deep with scuba diving. After a day of sun and surf, pamper yourself at the Radisson Blu, and let your taste buds dance with culinary delights at Fisherman's Wharf and Martin's Corner, ensuring your Goan adventure is unforgettable.





ANDAMAN ISLANDS

Location

Andaman Islands

Activities

Snorkeling
Sea Walk
Kayaking

Best Time to Visit

Venture to the Andaman Islands between November and April, when the azure waters are at their calmest and clearest.

Time Required

Dedicate a day to these awe-inspiring watersports and immerse yourself in a world below the surface.

Entry Fee

Prices for underwater thrills start at INR 1500 for snorkeling, INR 3500 for the mesmerizing sea walk, and INR 1000 for the tranquil kayaking experience.

Accomodation

Embrace the tranquility of SeaShell, Havelock, where nature's beauty and modern comforts entwine.

Restaurants

Anju Coco Resto - Sample the island's diverse flavors with sumptuous seafood and mouthwatering vegetarian dishes.
Full Moon Café - A little haven of health and taste, offering organic treats to nourish your senses.

How to Reach

Fly into Port Blair, the gateway to Andaman, and sail away on a ferry to Havelock Island, where aquatic wonders await.

About

Welcome to the underwater kingdom of the Andaman Islands, where the ocean invites you to snorkel with vibrant marine life, walk amongst the fishes on the sea floor, and paddle your way through the pristine waters. At SeaShell, recharge your spirit for the next day's escapades, while Anju Coco Resto and Full Moon Café tantalize your taste buds with delightful island fare.





RISHIKESH

Location

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Activities

White Water Rafting
Kayaking
Cliff Jumping

Best Time to Visit

From late September to early June, Rishikesh promises wild waters and heart-pounding thrills.

Time Required

Set aside a day of adrenaline-pumping action as you conquer the untamed rapids and challenge yourself with kayaking and cliff jumping.

Entry Fee

Embrace the rush with packages starting at INR 1000 to INR 4500, depending on your chosen escapade.

Accomodation

Surrender to nature's embrace at Aloha on the Ganges, a riverside sanctuary like no other.

Restaurants

The Sitting Elephant - Savor delicious North Indian and Continental cuisine with the majestic river as your backdrop.
Chotiwala Restaurant - Indulge in authentic flavors while relishing the warm hospitality of Rishikesh.

How to Reach

Take the road less traveled as you embark on a 6-hour drive from Delhi or fly to Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun. From there, the adventure awaits.

About

Rishikesh, the adrenaline capital of India, invites you to take the plunge into its untamed waters. Brace yourself for the thrill of white water rafting, paddle through the swirling rapids, and leap into the abyss with cliff jumping. At Aloha on the Ganges, bask in the serenity of the riverside sanctuary, while The Sitting Elephant and Chotiwala Restaurant tantalize your taste buds with the flavors of the north. Prepare for a day of exhilarating escapades and unforgettable memories amidst the roaring beauty of Rishikesh!
