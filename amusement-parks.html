IMAGICA THEME PARK

Location

Mumbai, Maharashtra

Best Time to Visit

Explore the enchanting Imagica Theme Park between November and February, when Mumbai's weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor adventures.

Time Required

Plan for an action-packed day to relish every ride and attraction.

Entry Fee

The entry fee starts at around INR 1000 for adults and INR 800 for children, offering a gateway to a world of fantasy and excitement.

Accomodation

Retreat to Novotel Imagica Khopoli, a whimsical resort just a stone's throw away from the park, offering a magical stay.

Restaurants

Arrmada - Savor delectable Indian and international dishes while enjoying an extraordinary dining experience.
Zeze Bar + Grill - A vibrant poolside restaurant serving lip-smacking grills and refreshing beverages.

How to Reach

Located in Khopoli, Imagica Theme Park is approximately a 2-hour drive from Mumbai. Taxis and buses are readily available for a hassle-free journey.

About

Enter the realm of imagination and thrill at Imagica Theme Park, where rides and attractions bring fairy tales to life. Spend a day filled with laughter, adventure, and joy, and then unwind at Novotel Imagica Khopoli, where magic lingers around every corner. Indulge your taste buds at Arrmada and Zeze Bar + Grill, adding an extra dose of delight to your unforgettable experience at Imagica.





WONDERLA

Location

Kochi, Kerala

Best Time to Visit

Dive into the wonders of Wonderla Kochi between September and February when Kerala's weather is pleasant, enhancing your theme park adventure.

Time Required

Set aside a full day for an exhilarating journey through the park's exhilarating rides and attractions.

Entry Fee

The entry fee for adults is around INR 800 on weekdays and INR 1000 on weekends. For children, it's approximately INR 650 on weekdays and INR 800 on weekends.

Accomodation

Unwind at The Gateway Hotel Marine Drive, offering a luxurious retreat after an action-packed day at Wonderla.

Restaurants

Black Pearl - Savor a delectable assortment of cuisines in an elegant setting, making your dining experience truly memorable.
Oceanos - Dive into coastal delicacies while enjoying breathtaking views of the sea.

How to Reach

Wonderla Kochi is easily accessible by road and is located about 15 kilometers from Kochi city center. Taxis and buses are available for a comfortable ride.

About

Wonderla Kochi promises a day filled with wonder and thrills, where adrenaline takes center stage. Brace yourself for an adventure of a lifetime with family and friends, and then indulge in the luxury of The Gateway Hotel Marine Drive. Relish the culinary delights at Black Pearl and Oceanos, adding a touch of gourmet magic to your journey through Wonderla Kochi.





ADLABS IMAGICA

Location

Khopoli, Maharashtra

Best Time to Visit

Surrender to the charm of Adlabs Imagica between October and March, when the weather is mild and inviting for an enjoyable experience.

Time Required

Immerse yourself in a full day of fun, laughter, and enchantment as you explore the captivating world of Adlabs Imagica.

Entry Fee

The entry fee ranges from INR 1299 to INR 1499 for adults, and INR 1099 to INR 1299 for children, depending on the selected package.

Accomodation

Experience the magic of Novotel Imagicaa Khopoli, an enchanting resort located close to the theme park, offering a delightful stay.

Restaurants

Roberto's Food Court - Delight in a diverse range of cuisines and relish the moments of joy with every bite.
The Imagicaa Capital - An exquisite dining destination where flavors from around the world unite.

How to Reach

Situated in Khopoli, Adlabs Imagica is approximately a 2-hour drive from Mumbai. Easily accessible by road, taxis and buses are readily available for a convenient journey.

About

Step into a world of enchantment and excitement at Adlabs Imagica, where every corner invites you to embark on a magical adventure. Spend a day exploring thrilling rides and captivating attractions before retiring to the comforting embrace of Novotel Imagicaa Khopoli. Feast your senses at Roberto's Food Court and The Imagicaa Capital, adding culinary delight to your unforgettable experience at Adlabs Imagica.
